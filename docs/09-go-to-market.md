# 09 — Go-to-market (90-day plan)

> The first 90 days of Underline going public, week by week. Keeps cohort intake constraints (six retainers per month) front-and-center. The launch is deliberately quiet — a literary-agency announcement should not announce itself like a SaaS launch.

## Pre-launch (Day -7 to Day 0)

| Day | Owner | Task |
|---|---|---|
| -7 | Head writer | Voice profile draft for the founding-team cohort (4 of 6 slots taken by founder-circle peers who have been waiting) |
| -7 | Chief of Design | Final QA of `linkedin-b2b-organic.prin7r.com` — desktop + mobile screenshots, manuscript frame typography, contrast audit |
| -5 | Ops | Deploy to `storage-contabo`, Traefik labels verified, NOWPayments hosted-invoice end-to-end test (real $1 invoice → cancel) |
| -5 | Head writer | Send 12 hand-written intro emails to peer-network operators, with the landing URL and one paragraph of context. NO calls-to-action. |
| -3 | Head writer | Draft cohort-1 onboarding kit (Telegram channel templates, voice-intake recording deck) |
| -1 | Head writer | Sign two cohort-1 retainers verbally, hold their NOWPayments invoices to send on Day 0 |
| 0 | Head writer | Day-0 post on the head writer's personal LinkedIn (200 words, not 2,000): *"I'm starting Underline. Six retainers per cohort. We close intake the first Monday of every month. Here's the URL. Here's the anti-feature list. Don't tag me, don't 'congrats!' me. Read the list."* |

## Month 1 — cohort 1 (six retainers)

### Week 1 (Day 1-7)
- **Mon (Day 1)** — Open intake. Two retainers from peer-network warm list pay invoices Day 1. Two intake calls scheduled for Day 2 (also from the warm list).
- **Tue (Day 2)** — Two intake calls. Both convert. Cohort: 4 of 6.
- **Wed-Fri** — Three more intake calls from the Day-0 post (it surfaces in 2,400 feeds; ~6 messages from cold inbound). Cohort 6/6 by Friday end.
- **Friday (Day 5) 18:00 Lisbon** — Close intake for Cohort 1. Wait-list opens for Cohort 2. Wait-list page is a single sentence: *"Cohort 1 closed. Cohort 2 opens June 1. Email writers@linkedin-b2b-organic.prin7r.com to be added."*

### Week 2 (Day 8-14)
- All six retainers complete Voice intake calls (90 min each, head writer).
- Voice profiles drafted — 12 pages each. Head writer drafts; senior writer reviews.

### Week 3 (Day 15-21)
- First posts ship — six principals, each posts on Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday. ~4 of 6 land their first posts cleanly; 2 retainers need an additional voice-tuning round.
- Comment-engine kicks off — 50 target accounts × 6 retainers = 300 accounts under daily watch. The head writer + 2 senior writers handle the comment queue.

### Week 4 (Day 22-30)
- First monthly accuracy report drafted.
- Annual-prepay conversation with the cohort: 1-2 retainers convert.
- One asynchronous "how it's going" check-in by Telegram with each retainer.
- Day 30: First-month NOWPayments renewal invoices issued automatically. ~5 of 6 renew (one cancellation = expected; the cancellation conversation is recorded as a learning).

## Month 2 — cohort 2 (six retainers)

### Week 5 (Day 31-37)
- Open intake. Wait-list (~14 names) gets the heads-up 48 hours before the 1st-Monday open. Three of them convert Day 1.
- Three additional cold inbounds from organic discovery (the cohort-1 ghostwritten posts now have 4 weeks of history visible to the audience).
- Cohort 6/6 by Wednesday this time. Close intake Wednesday 18:00 Lisbon. Two-day-early close communicated as a single-line update.

### Week 6 (Day 38-44)
- Cohort-2 voice intakes.
- First Stratechery-style internal write-up published in the GTM Partners Slack: *"What we changed when our comment dwell time went from 2 to 9 minutes."* (~120 words, no link to the landing.)

### Week 7 (Day 45-51)
- Cohort-2 first posts ship.
- Cohort-1 retainers enter month 2 — comment-engine relationships start to surface (a few warm DM threads).

### Week 8 (Day 52-60)
- Two cohort-1 DMs become discovery calls (the recurring-use moment from `docs/03-user-journeys.md`).
- One cohort-1 retainer converts to annual prepay (~$2k discount baked in, two months free).
- Day 60: cohort-1 month-2 renewals. ~5 of 5 remaining renew.

## Month 3 — cohort 3 + 90-day review

### Week 9 (Day 61-67)
- Open intake. Wait-list now ~22 names; Day-1 conversions are 4 of 6. Cohort fills Day 2.
- Two of the cohort-3 retainers come from cohort-1 referrals (the first observed referral conversions).

### Week 10 (Day 68-74)
- Cohort-3 voice intakes.
- Two of the founder-team-network cohort-1 retainers introduce Underline to their lead investor's portfolio CMO. One investor meeting books for week 12.

### Week 11 (Day 75-81)
- Cohort-3 first posts ship.
- Cohort-1 retainers reach 90 days; second annual-prepay conversation; 1 more conversion.
- Cohort-2 retainers reach 60 days; first DM-becomes-meeting events surface.

### Week 12 (Day 82-90)
- 90-day review:
  - **Cohort fill rate**: 18/18 (100%).
  - **Renewal rate at month 1**: 5/6 (83%).
  - **Renewal rate at month 2**: 5/5 (100%).
  - **Annual prepay conversions**: 3/18 (17%).
  - **Average time from cohort open to close**: 2.7 working days.
  - **Wait-list conversion to cohort 4**: ~12 of 22 (55%).

- Investor meeting (Week 12). One portfolio intro pipeline opens for Q2.

## Launch sequence (the only public event)

The "launch" is **not a launch**.

- **Day 0 LinkedIn post** — the head writer's personal account. 200 words. No celebration emojis. No "I'm so excited to share."
- **Day 0 email to 12 peer-network operators** — written by hand, no template, no CRM merge fields.
- **Day 0 commit + push to GitHub** — the public landing repo goes from private to public.
- **Day 0 Notion update** — the opportunity in `prin7r-projects` Notion is moved Stage `Lead → Qualified` and stamped with the deploy URL.

That is the entire launch.

## Quarter-2 plan summary (Day 91-180)

(Out of scope for the 90-day plan, listed for continuity.)

- Open the founder-circle peer wait-list (estimated 50+ names by Day 91).
- Add a guest-spot per cohort (one 5-week trial retainer for a credible non-paying founder who agrees to write publicly about the experience).
- First "Underline house style" essay published as `/notes` on the subdomain (one piece of tradecraft per quarter).
- Hire third senior writer (full-time, Lisbon-based). Cohort cap rises to 8 in Q3 once the third writer is shipping cleanly.

## Risks tracked weekly

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Cohort doesn't fill | Wait-list is the leading indicator; if Cohort 2 day-3 has fewer than 4/6, the head writer makes 6 hand-written referral asks |
| First-month churn > 1/6 | Voice-intake quality is the load-bearing variable; we'd add a +30 min intake call mid-month-1 to stabilize |
| NOWPayments rate-limit / KYC pause | Plisio backup is stubbed; takes ~2h to bring online if the primary trips |
| Head writer over-extended | Hard cap on 16 retainers under management; if exceeded, intake closes early next cohort |
| Pavilion / GTM Partners removes us for "self-promotion" | Accept; do not appeal; pivot to a smaller invite-only Slack |
