# 07 — Sales strategy

> Sales motion is **founder-led, retainer-capped, intake-cohorted**. We don't run a sales team and we won't until we exceed $4M ARR. The motion is high-touch, low-volume, asymmetric upside. Pricing is non-negotiable.

## Motion

**Founder-led + senior-writer-led.** The head writer (one person, full-time, also the agency principal) runs every intake call. Every. Single. One. This is structural — the head writer is the editorial filter and the cohort gatekeeper. Delegating intake to a salesperson would lose the editorial signal and over-fill the cohort.

**Retainer-capped.** Six new retainers per monthly cohort. This is a hard ceiling. We turn business away every cohort and we publish the wait-list. Cap is enforced by the head writer's calendar, not by a CRM.

**Intake-cohorted.** Intake closes the first Monday of every month at 18:00 Lisbon time. We accept calls until that hour, then we close the cohort and start the wait-list for next month. The cadence is communicated on the landing and in every email reply.

## Why this motion (not a SaaS-style sales team)

| Lever | Underline | A typical SaaS sales team |
|---|---|---|
| Channel | High-trust referral + organic discovery | Outbound + inbound at scale |
| Sales cycle | 4-21 days | 30-180 days |
| Conversion | 70% call → retainer (referral), 35% (cold inbound) | 20-30% MQL → close |
| CAC | ~$320 fully loaded | $1.5k-15k |
| LTV | $35-90k (avg 13-month retention) | varies |
| Sales team cost | $0 (head writer is also the founder) | $4-15k/closed retainer |

The math: a sales team would 1.6× our top-of-funnel and crush the editorial filter that makes the brand work. The unit economics get *worse* with a sales team, not better.

**Discounts.** None. Annual prepay = two months free (the only discount we offer, applied as a one-time credit at month 11 and 12). No coupons, no promos, no first-month-50%-off.

**Setup fee.** None. The first month is the test.

**Cancellation.** Any month, by email, no exit interview. We refund the in-flight month if the customer has not received their first post yet. No prorating.

**Annual prepay terms.** Two months free, but the customer signs a 12-month commitment with mutual cancellation clauses. ~25% of cohort takes annual prepay by month 4.

## Why these specific prices

The Operator tier is anchored at $2,990 to be:
- Below the noise threshold for a CFO approval ($3k = under most expense-policy thresholds).
- Above the "ghostwriter" market median ($1.5-2k) by enough to signal the productized service.
- Well below an internal hire's loaded cost ($90k+ /yr for a head of content).
- Below the $5k threshold where most CFOs require multi-vendor procurement review.

Founder ($1,490) is the on-ramp — for the founder who isn't ready for two calls/week. We don't market it as cheap; we market it as "if you only have 30 minutes/week to talk to a writer."

Director ($4,990) prices for the second-time founder with a Series-B clock — the daily editorial slot is the load-bearing feature. We expect 1-2 Directors per cohort.

## Objection handling — the seven we hear most

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Will my voice actually sound like me?" | Yes — that's the work. 90-min Voice intake (recorded), 12-page voice profile, every post sits in your Telegram for sign-off. If you don't recognise yourself in the writing after month one, we refund the month. |
| "Why crypto? My CFO is going to ask." | NOWPayments invoice in USDT/USDC on a hosted page. Receipt is auto-generated, account-payable-friendly. If your CFO blocks it, we hand-wire a USD wire — happens for ~10% of retainers. |
| "Can you do less than the Founder tier? Just one post a week?" | No. One post/week breaks rhythm; you'd churn at month two. We've tried. |
| "Are you using AI to write my posts?" | We use AI to draft outlines and pull from transcripts. Final drafts are written by a human (the head writer or one of two senior writers). Comments are 100% human. We say so on the page and we mean it. |
| "Why is the comment-engine so expensive when Taplio is $40/mo?" | Because Taplio's comments are LLM-generated and detectable. Ours are by hand, 9-min average dwell time, signed off by you. The cost is the writer-hour. |
| "What if my voice is technical / regulated / niche?" | We have retainers in fintech, SOC2-audited platforms, regulated industries. Compliance pre-clearance happens at intake. We've turned away healthcare-diagnostic and securities-recommendation work. |
| "What's your exit clause?" | Cancel any month, by email. We don't auto-renew if you missed the previous month's editorial call. We refund the in-flight month if you haven't received your first post yet. |

## Sales artefacts (the only ones we ship)

- **The landing** — `linkedin-b2b-organic.prin7r.com`. Written like the Stratechery email, not like a SaaS marketing page.
- **The intake call** — 30 minutes; head writer; no slides; covers the qualifying criteria from `docs/05-audience-profile.md` plus the two contested-POV check.
- **The voice intake (post-payment)** — 90-min call, recorded.
- **The signed contract** — one page, plain English, includes the anti-feature manifesto verbatim, names the head writer and the assigned senior writer, names the cohort intake date.

We do not produce: case-study PDFs, proposal decks, capability statements, comparison spreadsheets, ROI calculators, or "value engineering" worksheets. The closest analogue is the literary-agency author intake — we work the way agents work, not the way SaaS sells.

## Funnel metrics (target)

```
100   site visits
 18   click "Take retainer →"  OR  "Book a voice call"
  9   complete intake call (50% no-show, by design — the email is friction)
  4   become retainer (44%)
  3   complete month 1 (75%)
  2.7 still on retainer at month 6 (90% month-on-month retention)
  1.9 still on retainer at month 13 (annualised LTV anchor)
```

Average LTV per retainer at the Operator tier: **$38,800** at $2,990 × 13 months. Cohort gross margin (after writer cost, infrastructure, NOWPayments fee): **~58%**.

## What we'll change in 2027

- **Add a 4th tier "Studio" at $9,990/mo.** Two-team retainer for sales-led B2B teams running 4 voices in parallel. Currently we sell this as a one-off custom; productizing it as a tier opens the cohort fairly.
- **Run two cohorts per month** (1st and 15th) once the senior writer team is at four FTE. Doubles capacity without breaking the cap.
- **Open a single guest-spot per cohort** — a 5-week trial retainer for a non-paying highly-credible founder who agrees to publicly write about the experience. Marketing flywheel.
